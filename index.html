About Me

Hello! I'm currently a rising junior at Amherst College double majoring in computer science and mathematics. I'm interested in software engineering and data science, and I am very fortunate to have interned as a software engineer for the past two summers. Most of my experience is in backend-end development, but I've recently been improving my skills in data science and exploring ways in which it can help drive social impact.

Since my first year at Amherst, I've been working for the computer science department as both a teaching assistant and a peer tutor for multiple computer science courses. I am also currently the co-president of Amherst's Computer Science Club. Outside of academics, I've held leadership positions in Amherst's Asian Students Association as co-chair of the Asian Men's Collective and as treasurer of the Chinese Student Association.

I'm actively seeking internship opportunities for Summer 2021, so if you know of any, I would love to learn about them!
